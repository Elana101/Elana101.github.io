Performing Experience

In 2012, park Caiying won the first place in YG Australia trials, and in the same year, she joined the company and became a trainee. On September 15, park Caiying participated in the feat of "without you" in G-Dragon's first mini album "one of a kind".

On the 28th day of the year, South Korea hit the No. 1 music combination of "last's" in iTunes, which took more than 18 months to release. On July 20, the group held its first overseas showcase in Japan's Takeshima Museum, officially announcing its debut in Japan. On August 30, with the Group officially released the Japanese Debut Music Album "blackpink". On October 29, he sang the theme song just dance for jtbc variety show mixnine. In November, the single "as if it's your last" sung with the group was selected as the interlude by Warner Bros' action science fiction film "justice alliance".

In January 2018, the reality show variety show "blackpink house" with the combination premiered. On March 28, with the release of the first Japanese mini album "re: blackpink". On June 15, with the release of the mini album "square up", the album contains a variety of styles. At the same time, the album also sets a new record in the history of Korean pop music, in which female singers have won one of the most national charts. From July 24, the first Japanese arena Tour "blacklink arena tour 2018" will be held with the Group. September 23, with the release of the single "so hot". On October 25, she released her single kiss and make up, which was included in the album Dua LiPA.

In January, 2019, with the group, the "blacklink 2019 world tour with Kia [in your area]" world tour will be held in Bangkok. On April 5, with the release of the mini album "kill this love", the album refreshed the achievements of "square up" and entered the main list of British official company albums. On October 16, the Japanese version of the mini album "kill this love" was released with the group, and a number of activities started in Japan.

On May 28, 2020, with the release of the single "sour candy", which is a collaboration between the group and Lady Gaga, the song has refreshed the ranking of K-pop women's group songs and also become the best-selling song of the group. On June 13, he played in the reality show 24 / 365 with blackpink with the combination, which recorded their return and shared their daily life through variety shows; on June 26, he released the single "how you like that", which is the first regular album of the combination, and the song was on the official single list of the UK and billboard of the US HOT100 was ranked 20th and 33rd respectively, refreshing the history of K-pop women's group; on August 28, it released the cooperation single "ice cream" with Selena Gomez; on October 2, it released the official album "the album" with the combination.
